Gen AI Foundations Bootcamp for Startups

The Transformation Journey

From To
Executives uncertain where to begin with AI, conflicting priorities, wasted resources on disconnected experiments, and competitors pulling ahead. Clarity and confidence about the AI journey, leadership alignment, and excitement about quick wins showing measurable value within weeks.

Purpose

  • Provide startup leadership with practical Gen AI education focused on business applications
  • Identify high-impact, quick-win AI opportunities specific to your business model
  • Create an implementation roadmap that respects startup resource constraints
  • Develop decision frameworks for build vs. buy choices in AI implementation
  • Establish foundation for AI governance appropriate to startup scale

Deliverables

  • AI opportunity assessment customised to your specific business model
  • Strategic framework for evaluating new potential AI applications
  • Prioritised use case roadmap focusing on quick wins and ROI
  • Build vs. buy decision framework for AI implementation
  • 30-day follow-up session to review progress and address challenges

Investment

£6,000 for a 2-day bootcamp or £8,000 for 3-day bootcamp (per company)

Workshop Overview

Below is a high-level structure of our 3-day workshop:

Day 1: Foundations & Discovery

  • Morning: Welcome, Gen AI landscape overview, hands-on tool introduction
  • Afternoon: Identifying AI opportunities in your business, prompt engineering fundamentals

Day 2: Implementation & Integration

  • Morning: Advanced prompting strategies, specialised implementation tracks (choose Content Creation, Customer Support, or Operations)
  • Afternoon: No-code/low-code tool workshop, guided implementation lab, integration case studies

Day 3: Scaling, Optimisation & Strategy

  • Morning: Success measurement, advanced implementation topics including data privacy and compliance
  • Afternoon: Strategic 90-day roadmap development, implementation plan presentations, future-proofing strategy

Each day includes interactive exercises, one-on-one coaching, and actionable outputs directly applicable to your business.
